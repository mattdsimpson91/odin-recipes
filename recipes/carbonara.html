Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 250gr Pasta
  • 75gr Pancetta
  • 2 tbs Olive Oil
  • 3 Eggs
  • 75gr freshly grated parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper

Method

  1. Start cooking pasta as per packet instructions
  2. Cook up your pancetta in a pan until it's nicely browned. Turn off heat.
  3. Whilst your pancetta is cooking, whisk eggs in a bowl and add 2/3rds of your parmesan cheese and a stack of pepper.
  4. Once the pasta is cooked, strain and toss it into pan with pancetta.
  5. After a few seconds, pour egg mixture over pasta and mix around ensuring that pasta is coated, but not scrambling the egg mixture.
  6. Serve up on a plate with added parmesan and black pepper. Done!
